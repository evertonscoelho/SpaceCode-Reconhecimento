Privacy policy

Before you start using Space Code, we suggest that you read this document till the end, where we explain how this app deal with yout data.

1. Device Camera

Space Code is an app that makes use of your device camera to allow users to take pictures of the pieces that are used in this game. those pictures are sent to a server that do not store them, but process them trying to identify the pieces present in the image. The algorithmn resposible for this tasks is incapable to identify anything else than pieces and do not store any information related to the results obtained.

This app does not access any other device fuctionality and does not store any personal data.
